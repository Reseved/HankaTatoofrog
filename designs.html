Designs

What to do to book a session with me:
1. Contact Me:
• Write to me via the social media links provided, briefly describing your idea, style, and the proposed placement of your tattoo.
• If you have any inspiration or reference images, please attach them to your message.
2. Discussing the Project and Choosing the Idea:
• We will discuss your idea through messages. If you are unsure about the placement, I will advise on where the design best fits anatomically.
• It is very important that you specify which elements should or should not be included if you already have a clear vision.
• After discussing the design idea, we will agree on a date and time for the tattoo session.
• A deposit or advance payment ranging from £20 to £50 is required.
3. Concept Development:
• Based on our discussion, I will create a sketch that reflects your idea and send it to you for review via social media.
• If necessary, we will make adjustments based on your feedback.
4. Session Execution:
• You will receive preparation recommendations in advance, and during the session, I will bring the approved design to life while following all sanitary standards.
5. After the Session:
• You will be provided with aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing.
• If necessary, minor corrections can be discussed remotely.
